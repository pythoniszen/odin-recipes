Pepperoni Pizza

Description

The good old fashioned meal everybody loves.

  • Dough
  • Flower
  • Olive Oil
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Basil
  1. Stretch pizza dough.
  2. Add pizza dough to lightly oiled pizza stone.
  3. Add pizza sauce.
  4. Add toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Top with fresh parmesan and basil.
