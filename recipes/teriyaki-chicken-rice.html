Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowl

Description

This type of meal is known as donburi, which translates to bowl in English. Donburi comes from Japan and contains meat over steamed rice. Chicken teriyaki don is the perfect iteration of this concept. It maintains a simple yet classic and undeniably supreme dining experience.

  • Rice
  • Chicken Thigh
  • Garlic Powder
  • Mirin
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sake
  • Ginger
  • Pepper
  • Kosher Salt
  • Oil
  • Green Onion
  1. Prepare rice on stove or in rice cooker.
  2. Mix the teriyaki using soy sauce, sake, mirin, and sugar. Grate the ginger into the mix and whisk well.
  3. Tenderize the chicken and season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  4. Cook the chicken on a pan using oil.
  5. Cut the chicken and assemble the bowl using the remaining ingredients.
